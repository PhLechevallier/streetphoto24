<p></br>If want to be sure to see swans in the middle of the city, go to Almere (Netherlands). You will walk along the channels in the downtown and you will see them. This swans are always in the Olstgracht at a few hundreds meters from the shops (Olst Channel). Sometimes they are romantic. </p>
