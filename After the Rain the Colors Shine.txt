<p></br>The colors are much more shiny after the rain! Even when the weather is fine the old graffiti and the old decorations are often dull. A bit rain and then a little sunshine are enough to get all colors alive again. I took these photos in the streets and in the parks near the station of Muziekwijk in Almere after a rainfall.</p>
