<p></br>Mosaics, "Time == Art" in the middle of the Belfort Square in Almere. The design of these clock and banks is inspired by the Mayan culture. The Mayans considered time and energy as art. Emeke de Buitelaar, the artist, explains: "<em>We can not ignore the time in a time where time is money. The Mayans live by: energy x time is art. That seems like a much better starting point.</em>"</p>
